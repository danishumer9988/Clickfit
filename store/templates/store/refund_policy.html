Return & Refund Policy

We want you to be happy with your purchase! That's why we offer a 7-day return policy on all tech products. If you are not satisfied for any reason, you can return the product for a full refund.

How to Return a Product

  • Contact us within 7 days of receiving your order.
  • Provide your order number and the reason for the return.
  • We will send you a return shipping label.
  • Pack the product securely and send it back using the provided label.
  • Once we receive the returned product, your refund will be processed within 10 business days.

Exceptions to the Return Policy

The following items are not eligible for return:

  • Products that have been used or damaged.
  • Products missing parts or accessories.
  • Products purchased on sale or clearance.

Refunds

Refunds will be processed to the original payment method:

  • If you paid by credit card, the refund will be credited back to your account.
  • If you paid via Bank, JazzCash, or Easypaisa, the refund will be sent to your account.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at Support@clickfit.store.

Thank you for shopping with Click Fit!
