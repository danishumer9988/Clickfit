Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q. Is ordering online secure?

A. Yes. We take the utmost care with the information you provide during the ordering process. Your data is handled securely and only accessible to authorized staff. For further questions, email us at support@clickfit.store.

Q. How do I place my order?

A.

  1. Click on the product you want to buy.
  2. Click on the “Add To Cart” button.
  3. Add multiple products by repeating this process.
  4. Click “Cart” from the menu.
  5. Click “Proceed to Checkout.”
  6. Fill in billing details and check your order summary.
  7. Click the “Place Order” button.
  8. You will receive a confirmation email.
  9. Cash On Delivery (COD) is available.

Q. How do I cancel an order on ClickFit.Store?

A. Login to your account and cancel your order from the dashboard. Guest users can call our helpline during office hours or cancel during the final confirmation call.

Q. What is your refund policy?

A. Please visit our Refund Policy page.

Q. What is your shipping cost?

A. We offer Free Shipping. Our shipping cost is Rs 00/-.

Q. Which courier service do you use for shipping?

A. We use M & P, TCS, and Leopards Courier Services for delivery.

Q. How long does delivery take?

A. We deliver within 2-3 working days after the confirmation call.

Q. How can I order an out-of-stock product?

A. You can pre-order out-of-stock items by contacting us via call or email.
