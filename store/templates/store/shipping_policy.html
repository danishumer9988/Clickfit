Shipping Policy

Thank you for shopping with Click Fit Store! We are committed to providing you with a seamless and enjoyable shopping experience. Please review our shipping policy below:

Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 1 day after payment confirmation. Weekends and holidays may affect processing times, but we strive to prepare your order as quickly as possible.

Shipping Method

We offer standard shipping for all orders through reliable courier partners. Once your order is shipped, you will receive a confirmation message with tracking information to monitor your delivery.

Delivery Time

Delivery usually takes 2 to 3 business days after the order has been shipped. Please note that weather conditions or other unforeseen factors may occasionally cause delays.

Shipping Destinations

Currently, we ship orders within Pakistan only. If you're outside of Pakistan and interested in placing an order, please contact our support team to discuss possible arrangements.

Shipping Costs

Good news! We offer free shipping on all orders within our shipping areas. There are no additional charges for standard shipping.

Order Tracking

Once your order has been dispatched, you will receive a message containing your tracking number. Use this number to track your package in real-time and receive updates on delivery status.

Shipping Address

Please ensure your shipping address is accurate and complete. We are not responsible for orders delivered to incorrect addresses. If you need to make changes, contact us immediately after placing your order.

Order Confirmation

You will receive an order confirmation email shortly after placing your order. If you do not receive this email, check your spam folder or contact our support team.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our shipping policy, feel free to reach out to our customer support team at Support@talhareviews.store.

Thank you for choosing Click Fit Store! We appreciate your business.

Sincerely,
Click Fit Store Team
