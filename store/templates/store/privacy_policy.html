Privacy Policy

At Click Fit Store, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

When you place an order, sign up for our newsletter, or contact us, we may collect the following information:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Shipping and billing address
  • Payment details (processed securely)

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Process and deliver your orders
  • Send order confirmations and shipping updates
  • Respond to customer service requests
  • Send promotional emails (if you have opted in)

Data Security

We use secure payment gateways and protect your personal information with modern security technologies. Your data is stored safely and is accessible only by authorized personnel.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with trusted partners strictly for order processing, delivery, or legal compliance.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve your browsing experience. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of promotional communications at any time

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page, so please review it periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy policy, please contact us at Support@clickfit.store.
